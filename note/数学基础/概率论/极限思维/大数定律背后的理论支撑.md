### 样本数越多，估计越准

* 样本均值的方差：var[Mn]
  * ![image-20230404172340796](%E5%A4%A7%E6%95%B0%E5%AE%9A%E5%BE%8B%E8%83%8C%E5%90%8E%E7%9A%84%E7%90%86%E8%AE%BA%E6%94%AF%E6%92%91.assets/image-20230404172340796.png)
